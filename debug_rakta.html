    E-SAQR CARD

    To pay the transportation fees through the internal public buses

    Blue Card

    For all categories and users

    Card purchase value 30 AED including the 20 dirhams for the e-wallet value for the first time.
    Silver Card

    University and school students Senior citizens and residents (above 60 years old)

    Card purchase value 20 AED including 50% discount on the trip fees.
    Gold Card

    People of determination category

    Lifetime Free Card
    How to complete the transaction
    • 1 – The customer goes to the ticket counter at the main bus station
    • 2 – Requesting the card type (blue-gold-silver) from the station employee
    • 3 – Pay the fees
    • 4 – Receive the card
    Card sales points
    Blue card
    • 1 – Inside the bus
    • 2 – Ticket counter at the main bus station
    Gold and Silver card
    • 1 – Ticket counter at the main bus station
